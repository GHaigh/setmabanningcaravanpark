Welcome to Setmabanning Farm

Caravanning and Camping in the Heart of the Lake District

Setmabanning is a beautifully located family run caravan and camping park with stunning views of Blencathra and surrounding fells. We offer an excellent base for both exploration or simply relaxation.

The more energetic can tackle the Blencathra, Skiddaw and Helvellyn ranges which are all located nearby, or if you fancy something a little easier, there are beautiful footpaths and cycle tracks around the local valley which can also lead to Keswick.
